# Membership

Membership of Tiffany's Tales Book Club costs only £10 a month.

For that monthly membership you become part of the pack: you join a local in-person pack that meets once a month to discuss the book, you can take part in the everyday online chat in between meetings, and you read along with the monthly Book of the Month.

Joining is easy — use the "Join my pack today!" link on the website to sign up. If you'd like to ask anything before joining, the contact page has a form to get in touch, and we're always happy to welcome new readers.

Members are not just attendees — they help shape the club. You're welcome to suggest books for the club to read, so the reading list stays diverse and engaging.
