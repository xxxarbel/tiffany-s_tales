# About Tiffany's Tales Book Club

Tiffany's Tales is a cosy, in-person book club based in Maidstone, Kent, in the United Kingdom. It was started by Riette, who loves the idea of a book club and being part of an online club, but missed interacting with real people. At Tiffany's Tales, we believe in the power of stories to unite and inspire.

Our book club is a vibrant hub where bookworms from all walks of life come together to celebrate the written word. From thought-provoking book discussions to laid-back chatting about nothing in particular, our community fosters a love for reading and a real sense of belonging. Whether you seek literary recommendations, lively debates, or simply a place to express your passion for books, Tiffany's Tales is your literary sanctuary.

The brand has a friendly "pack" theme — the logo is a little white chihuahua sitting on an open book — so members are part of the pack. The club's invitation is simple and warm: "Join my pack today!"

We're a welcoming community for readers of all kinds. Come and explore the boundless joys of reading and forge meaningful connections through the pages of unforgettable tales.
