# About Tiffany's Tales Book Club

Tiffany's Tales is a cosy, in-person book club based in Maidstone, Kent, in the United Kingdom. It was started by Riette Beling, who loves the idea of a book club and was part of an online one, but missed interacting with real people. At Tiffany's Tales, we believe in the power of stories to unite and inspire.

The founder, Riette Beling, says that aside from her wonderful family, the two great loves of her life are books and dogs. She shares her home with Killer, her bold black Miniature Pinscher, and Tiffany, her fearless white Chihuahua. The club is named after Tiffany — she is the spark that inspired all of this, brave, independent, and unapologetically herself. The club is really about lively, joyful conversations about both stories and pets: after all, what could be better than curling up with a good book and a loyal dog by your side? One day Riette hopes to host a book and pet meetup, where tails wag and pages turn together.

Our book club is a vibrant hub where bookworms from all walks of life come together to celebrate the written word. From thought-provoking book discussions to laid-back chatting about nothing in particular, our community fosters a love for reading and a real sense of belonging. Whether you seek literary recommendations, lively debates, or simply a place to express your passion for books, Tiffany's Tales is your literary sanctuary.

The brand has a friendly "pack" theme — the logo is a little white chihuahua sitting on an open book — so members are part of the pack. The club's invitation is simple and warm: "Join my pack today!"

We're a welcoming community for readers of all kinds. Come and explore the boundless joys of reading and forge meaningful connections through the pages of unforgettable tales.
