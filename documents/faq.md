# Frequently Asked Questions

## How much does it cost?

Membership is only £10 a month.

## How often do you meet?

We meet once a month, in person, for book discussions, and we have an online group where members chat every day in between meetings.

## Where are you based?

We're based in Maidstone, Kent, in the United Kingdom. There are two packs you can join: the Maidstone Pack and the Sittingbourne Pack.

## Can I suggest a book for discussion?

Yes — we welcome book suggestions from our members to ensure diverse and engaging discussions.

## How do I join?

Use the "Join my pack today!" link on the website to become a member, or get in touch through the contact form if you have any questions first.

## What kind of books do you read?

All sorts — our Book of the Month ranges from page-turning thrillers to heart-warming romances and everything in between. You can browse the club's reading shelves and members' reviews on the site.
