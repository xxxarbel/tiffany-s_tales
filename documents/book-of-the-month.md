# Book of the Month

Each month the pack chooses a Book of the Month — a story we all read and discuss together. Our picks range widely, from page-turning thrillers to heart-warming romances and everything in between, so there's always something fresh to enjoy.

The idea is simple: grab a copy, find a comfy chair, and join the conversation. At the monthly in-person meeting we talk through that month's read, and in between we keep chatting about it online.

Members can suggest books for discussion, so the Book of the Month reflects what the pack is excited to read. We welcome suggestions to keep our discussions diverse and engaging.

If you'd like to know exactly what the club is reading right now, just ask the voice guide for "this month's read" and it will tell you the current pick from the club's reading shelves.
