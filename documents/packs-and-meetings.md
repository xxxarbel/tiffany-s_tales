# Packs and Meetings

Tiffany's Tales has two packs — local gatherings you can choose between:

- The Maidstone Pack is the home of Tiffany's Tales. Friendly faces, lively discussion, and a fresh read every month, right here in Maidstone, Kent. The Maidstone Pack meets the first Wednesday of every month at 7:30pm at The Muggleton Inn, 8–9 High Street, Maidstone, ME14 1HJ.
- The Sittingbourne Pack meets in a cosy local spot — settle in with a warm drink and good company while we talk books. The Sittingbourne Pack meets the first Monday of every month at 7:30pm at The Jenny Wren, Staplehurst Road, Sittingbourne, ME10 5TA.

Each pack meets once a month, in person, for our book discussions. Both packs cost the same £10 a month — pick the one nearest you. Between meetings, members chat every day in our Discord group, so the conversation never really stops.

Pick the gathering that feels like home — that's how you "find your pack". Both packs are warm, welcoming, and open to readers of all kinds.
