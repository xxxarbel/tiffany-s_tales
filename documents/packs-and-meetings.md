# Packs and Meetings

Tiffany's Tales has two packs — local gatherings you can choose between:

- The Maidstone Pack is the home of Tiffany's Tales. Friendly faces, lively discussion, and a fresh read every month, right here in Maidstone, Kent.
- The Sittingbourne Pack meets monthly in a cosy local spot — settle in with a warm drink and good company while we talk books.

Each pack meets once a month, in person, for our book discussions. Between meetings, members chat every day in our online group, so the conversation never really stops.

Pick the gathering that feels like home — that's how you "find your pack". Both packs are warm, welcoming, and open to readers of all kinds.
